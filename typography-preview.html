Font Comparison

Body Font: Inter

Inter is a clean, modern sans-serif typeface designed specifically for computer screens. It features excellent readability at all sizes and is optimized for UI design.

Weights: 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold)

Heading Font: Poppins

Poppins is a geometric sans-serif typeface that brings a modern, friendly feel to headings. Its rounded forms create visual interest while maintaining excellent legibility.

Weights: 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold), 800 (extrabold)

Font Weights

Medium (500) - Subtle emphasis

Semibold (600) - Strong emphasis

Bold (700) - Maximum emphasis
